Leased

Leased is a webapp that enables C2C transactions for all sorts of goods. Though focused on thrifting, users can choose to list and sell products of their choice. Users can take on the role of both a buyer and a seller. The webapp provides similar functionality to that of an online shop. Users get recommended products, perform product searches, check out their shopping carts and view their order management boards after purchase.

My aims for this project were pretty straightforward; it started off as a small project of mine to familarize with setting up a MERN app from scratch. I also wanted to focus on architecting the application, keeping in mind Design Patterns when implementing the code and keeping it clean. I further learnt to write Unit Tests with Jasmine, Mocha, Jest.

Technologies used: MongoDB, Express, React, Node (MERN) with Stripe payments processing functionality.

Codelaborate

Codelaborate a social platform with online judge for college students to pair program together in real-time.

This means that multiple users can edit the same piece of code, and changes are seen on-screen in real-time. This could be in the case of a practice programming assessment. Additional features include: voice & chat funtionalities (integrating WebRTC), note-taking during the session, and most importantly, a backend online judger (OJ) system for technical algorithm questions. It is supported with popular languages like Java, C++, Python.

Launched in beta in 2019, (originally Codelabs), Codelaborate scaled up to peak of 300+ MAU and 50 interviews conducted in a day.

Technologies used: Built with React, Node.js, MongoDB, Socket.io, Redis, RabbitMQ. Implemented a backend executor service with Python, Flask and Docker. Load balancing with Nginx, CI/CD pipeline with Jenkins and runs on AWS.

East Coast Beach Plan Bot

East Coast Beach Plan Bot is a chatbot built on Telegram's APIs to coordinate beach cleanups (#eastcoastbeachplan) in Singapore. The product is currently live and can be found here . The effort currently has around 2,000 volunteers with 500+ Monthly Active Users on the bot, with Singapore's Members of Parliament: Minister (Sustainability & Environment), Minister (National Development) and Senior Minister of State joining the initiative.
